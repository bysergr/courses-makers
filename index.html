What is Makers?

Makers is a talent incubator based in LATAM that focuses on nurturing and developing skills in entrepreneurship, coding, and business. Our mission is to empower the next generation of innovators and leaders in these field.

Why is Makers free?

Our program is free because we believe in making high-quality education and resources accessible to everyone. We are supported by partnerships, sponsorships, and grants that allow us to offer this opportunity at no cost to our participants.

Who can apply to Makers?

Our program is open to individuals who are passionate about entrepreneurship, coding, and business. We welcome applications from all backgrounds and skill levels, whether you are a beginner or an experienced professional looking to enhance your skills.

What are the criteria for selection?
We select participants based on their passion, potential, and commitment to learning and growing in the fields of entrepreneurship, coding, and business. We look for individuals who are motivated, curious, and eager to make an impact.
What happens after I complete the program?
After completing the program, you will have the skills, knowledge, and network to pursue your entrepreneurial ventures. Additionally, graduates will be offered a top internship in a high-growth startup in LATAM, providing invaluable real-world experience and opportunities for career advancement.
